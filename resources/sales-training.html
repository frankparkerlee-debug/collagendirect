Objection Handling

Common objections and how to overcome them with confidence.

Objection: "We don't have time for another system"

Response: "That's exactly why practices love CollagenDirect—it actually saves time. Our AI generates clinical notes in seconds, eliminating 15-20 minutes of documentation per patient. Plus, the platform generates additional revenue streams that more than justify the minimal time investment."

Objection: "Is this compliant? I'm worried about audits"

Response: "Compliance is built into every feature. Our AI ensures medical necessity documentation meets Medicare and commercial payer requirements. We provide automatic audit trails, and our compliance rate is 100%. In fact, practices often tell us they feel MORE protected with our documentation than manual methods."

Objection: "We're happy with our current supplier"

Response: "That's great! But here's what makes us different: we're not just a product supplier—we're a technology platform that helps you optimize clinical workflows and practice revenue. Our AI-powered documentation and billing optimization tools are unique in the industry. Can I show you a quick demo of how practices are generating $150k+ annually?"

Objection: "The revenue numbers sound too good to be true"

Response: "I understand your skepticism—let me break it down. With 50 wound care patients photographing wounds weekly, that's 200 photo reviews per month. At conservative reimbursement rates, that's $18k-$26k monthly, or $216k-$312k annually. Many practices are already seeing these results. Would you like to see our revenue calculator and detailed billing methodology?"
