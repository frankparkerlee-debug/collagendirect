Research Highlights

Collagen Dressings in Chronic Wounds

Meta-analyses and randomized studies show collagen dressings can accelerate healing in DFU, VLU, and pressure ulcers within comprehensive care plans.

Refs: Chaby 2007; Yang 2018.

Silver-Containing Collagen Composites

Reports of infection reduction and faster granulation vs. standard moist dressings, within antimicrobial stewardship protocols.

Refs: Lansdown 2002; Zhao 2019.

Human Allograft Matrices (Research)

Acellular dermal allografts provide structural cues aiding closure in complex wounds; controlled and cohort data available.

Refs: Mostow 2005; Serena 2009.
