CollagenDirect Portal — Facility Guide

For wound-care practices & facilities · Everything you need to order supplies and track shipments

Your CollagenDirect portal is where you add patients, place orders, and track shipments — all in one place. This guide walks through the everyday tasks. You'll find the navigation on the left side of every screen.

1Getting started

  1. Go to collagendirect.health and sign in with your email and password.
  2. First time? You'll be asked to review and accept the MD DME Product & Services Agreement and the Business Associate Agreement (BAA). Sign once and you're in.
  3. You'll land on your Dashboard — a snapshot of your patients, active orders, and recent activity.
Forgot your password? Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page. For help, contact orders@md-dme.com.

2Adding & finding patients

Open Patients from the sidebar.

  • Add a patient: click + New Patient and enter their details. Search is case-insensitive — type any part of a name to find someone.
  • Open a patient to see their information and order history.

3Placing orders

There are three order types, each on its own sidebar item. Pick the one that fits how the supplies are billed:

Patient Referral

Insurance-billed supplies for a specific patient (collagen and advanced dressings). Ships by the piece.

HealKit Order

Simple cash/wholesale supplies — no insurance paperwork. Ships to your office by the piece.

Wholesale Orders

Stock for your practice, billed to the facility and shipped by the box.

Patient Referral (insurance) order

  1. Open Patient Referral and choose (or add) the patient.
  2. Add the wound(s) and pick the product type and size, then set quantity, frequency, and duration.
  3. Attach any required documentation, sign, and submit.

HealKit order

  1. Open HealKit Order. New patients need only a first and last name — no insurance docs required.
  2. Confirm delivery (defaults to your office; choose another location if needed).
  3. Pick product type and size, set the quantity, sign, and submit.
What's the difference? HealKit skips insurance verification and ships straight to your office at simple wholesale pricing — great when you don't need to bill a patient's insurance. See the dedicated "Introducing HealKit" handout for details.

Wholesale order

  1. Open Wholesale Orders → Create.
  2. Wholesale orders ship to your facility (not a patient). Choose products and quantities (by the box) and submit.

4Tracking orders & shipments

Each order type has its own list (Patient Referral, HealKit Order, Wholesale Orders). Open a list to see status at a glance.

  • Status shows where each order is (Submitted → Approved → Shipped/In Transit → Delivered).
  • When an order ships, a Track link appears next to the status. Click it to open the carrier's tracking page (UPS). Expand the order or open Order Details to see the full tracking number.
  • Click any order to see its full detail — products, quantities, and shipping info.
Shipped notification: when we ship an order, you'll also get an email with a Track Package button.

5Photo Reviews

If your practice uses Photo Reviews, you can submit wound photos for clinical review and E/M billing support from the Photo Reviews page. Upload images and follow the prompts; reviewed encounters appear in your list.

6Messages, account & support

  • Messages — communicate with the CollagenDirect team about orders and questions.
  • Admin / Profile — update your practice details, locations, and team. Practice administrators can manage additional users.
  • Sign out from the menu when you're done.
