Introducing HealKit

HealKit is the easiest way to get the wound-care supplies your patients need — no insurance paperwork, no verification delays, and shipped straight to your office. You order what you need, we send it, and you're billed simple, transparent wholesale pricing. Making the switch takes about two minutes.

Why practices are switching to HealKit

No insurance paperwork

Skip the IVRs, prior auths, and benefit checks. Patient ID and clinical notes are optional, not required.

Shipped to your office

Orders default to your practice location. Need it somewhere else? Choose another delivery address per order.

Order in two minutes

Pick the patient, choose product type and size, set quantity — done. New patients need only a first and last name.

Pay for what you use

Billed by the actual piece at your wholesale price — not by forced full-box quantities.

    How to Order
    Get started in four steps

    Placing a HealKit order

    Log in to your CollagenDirect portal and select HealKit Order from the menu. The whole process takes about two minutes.

    1
    Choose the patientSearch an existing patient, or add a new one — only a first and last name are required.
    2
    Confirm deliveryOrders ship to your office by default. Select another location if you need supplies sent elsewhere.
    3
    Add the wound & productsPick the product type and size, then set quantity per change, frequency, and duration. Add as many wounds as needed.
    4
    Sign & submitYour name and credentials are pre-filled. Review and submit — that's it. We handle fulfillment and shipping.

    Ready to switch?

    You don't need to do anything special to start — just place your next supply order as a HealKit order. Want us to walk your team through it or move your standing orders over? We're happy to help.

    Email orders@md-dme.com  ·  or call your MD DME representative

    Good to know

    Still need insurance billing?

    For supplies that should bill to a patient's insurance, keep using a Referral order. HealKit is for practice-pay wholesale supply.

    Your pricing, applied automatically

    If your practice has negotiated wholesale pricing, HealKit uses it — across every login on your account.
