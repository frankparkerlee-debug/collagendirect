Sales Team User Guide

CollagenDirect — Sales Team Guide

For distributors & reps · Manage your clinics, orders, commissions, and team

Your sales portal is where you onboard clinics, watch their orders, and track your commissions. Distributors can also build and manage a team of reps. Sections marked Distributors apply only to distributor accounts.

1Signing in

  1. Go to collagendirect.health and sign in. Active sales accounts are taken straight to your sales dashboard.
  2. New rep? If a distributor invited you, open the invite email and click the link to set your password and sign your agreements. Your distributor activates your account — then you can log in.
Can't log in yet? If your status is still pending, your distributor (or the CollagenDirect team) needs to activate you. Reach out to whoever invited you.

2Your dashboard

The dashboard summarizes your book of business: clinics, physicians, orders this month, collected revenue, and commission earned. Distributors see these totals across their whole team (all of their reps' clinics); a rep sees their own accounts only.

3Managing clinics

Under My Clinics:

  • Clinic Roster — every clinic assigned to you, with patient and order counts. Search to find one fast.
  • Onboard New Clinic — set up a brand-new practice; it's automatically assigned to you.
  • Add Physician — add a provider to one of your practices.
  • My Assignment Requests — ask to be assigned to an existing clinic. The CollagenDirect team reviews and approves it.

4Orders & wholesale

  • Orders — every order from your clinics, with status and revenue. Filter by status or clinic.
  • Wholesale → Create Order — place a wholesale order for one of your wholesale-enabled clinics. View Orders lists them.
  • When an order ships, the status shows Shipped / In Transit with a Track link (UPS).

5Commissions & payouts

  • Commission Ledger — every commission you've earned, by order, with your running balance.
  • Payout History — payments you've received.

Commissions are calculated automatically when a clinic's payment is collected — there's nothing for you to submit.

6Building your teamDistributors

Distributors manage a team of reps. Each rep is tied to their own clinics and sees only their own accounts; you see everything across your team. Open My Reps.

Invite a rep

  1. Click + Invite Rep and enter their name, email, and phone.
  2. They receive an invite email, set a password, and sign their agreements.
  3. Back on My Reps, click Activate to turn on their login. (Use Resend Invite if their link expired, or Suspend to pause access.)
Each rep sees only their own clients. You always see the full picture — every rep, their clinics, orders, and earnings — from your dashboard and clinic roster.

7Setting your reps' commissionsDistributors

How the margin works: CollagenDirect pays you a per-item commission on each product. You decide how much of that to pass to each rep — and you keep the difference as your margin.

  1. On My Reps, click Commissions next to a rep.
  2. For each product you see your rate per item; enter what the rep earns per item (it can't exceed your rate).
  3. The page shows your margin per item as you type. Click Save Commissions.

From then on, when that rep's clinic places an order and payment is collected, the rep earns their per-item rate and you automatically earn the margin — both show in your commission ledgers.

Example: CollagenDirect pays you $90/box on a product. You set your rep at $60/box. On a 10-box order, the rep earns $600 and you earn $300.
