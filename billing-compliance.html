Medicare & Commercial Payer Compliance

Guidelines for compliant E/M billing for asynchronous wound photo reviews

‚úì Appropriate Use

Asynchronous telehealth wound photo reviews qualify for E/M billing when:

  • Clinical decision-making is documented for each review
  • Medical necessity is established (e.g., monitoring wound progression, adjusting treatment)
  • Documentation supports the level of service billed
  • Reviews are separate from CCM or other bundled services
  • Patient has provided consent for telehealth services
