DEFINITIONS

Protected Health Information (PHI)

PHI includes any individually identifiable health information transmitted or maintained in any form (electronic, paper, or oral) that relates to:

  • An individual's past, present, or future physical or mental health condition
  • The provision of health care to an individual
  • Payment for health care provided to an individual

Examples: Patient names, addresses, dates of birth, Social Security numbers, medical record numbers, insurance information, diagnoses, treatment plans, wound care documentation, photographs, and any other health-related data.
