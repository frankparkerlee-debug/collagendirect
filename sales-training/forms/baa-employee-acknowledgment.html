As a CollagenDirect employee, you are a "Business Associate" under HIPAA and will have access to Protected Health Information (PHI). This form confirms you understand your legal obligations.

Penalties for violations: Civil fines up to $50,000 per violation (up to $1.5M per year) and criminal penalties up to $250,000 and 10 years imprisonment.

Employee Information

Your Key Obligations as a Business Associate

1. Access Only When Necessary

Only access PHI when required for your job duties. Never access patient information out of curiosity.

2. Maintain Confidentiality

Never discuss patient information in public areas, on social media, or with family/friends.

3. Use Secure Communication

Use encrypted email only. Never send PHI via text, personal email, or unsecured methods.

4. Physical Security

Lock away documents, secure your workstation, shred PHI before disposal.

5. Report Breaches Immediately

Report any suspected breach or security incident within 24 hours to the Privacy Officer.

6. No Personal Devices

Never store PHI on personal phones, laptops, or cloud storage (Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.).
