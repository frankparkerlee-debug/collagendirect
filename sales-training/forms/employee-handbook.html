Again, welcome aboard. We're excited to work with you!

1. About CollagenDirect

Our Mission

To provide healthcare professionals with fast, compliant access to evidence-based collagen therapies that accelerate wound healing and improve patient outcomes.

Our Values

  • Patient First: Everything we do is focused on improving patient outcomes
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practices
  • Compliance: We maintain the highest standards of regulatory and HIPAA compliance
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our products, services, and processes
  • Teamwork: We collaborate, support each other, and celebrate shared success

2. Equal Employment Opportunity

CollagenDirect is an equal opportunity employer. We are committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state, or local law.

This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including recruiting, hiring, placement, promotion, termination, layoff, recall, transfer, leaves of absence, compensation, and training.

3. Employment Classification

Full-Time Employees

Employees who work 40 hours per week on a regular basis. Eligible for all company benefits.

Part-Time Employees

Employees who work less than 40 hours per week. Eligibility for benefits may vary.

Exempt vs. Non-Exempt

Exempt: Salaried employees not entitled to overtime pay under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA).
Non-Exempt: Hourly employees entitled to overtime pay for hours worked over 40 in a workweek.

4. Work Hours & Attendance

Standard Business Hours

Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (local time). Specific work schedules may vary by department and role.

Attendance Policy

Regular and punctual attendance is essential. If you will be late or absent, notify your supervisor as soon as possible, preferably before your scheduled start time.

Time Tracking

Non-exempt employees must accurately record all time worked using the company's time tracking system. Falsification of time records may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

5. Compensation & Payroll

Pay Schedule

Employees are paid bi-weekly via direct deposit. Pay stubs are available electronically through the payroll system.

Overtime

Non-exempt employees will be paid overtime at 1.5 times their regular rate for all hours worked over 40 in a workweek. Overtime must be pre-approved by your supervisor.

Sales Commissions

Sales employees may be eligible for commission-based compensation as outlined in their individual employment agreements or commission plans.

6. Benefits

Full-time employees are eligible for the following benefits (subject to plan terms and waiting periods):

  • Health Insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage
  • 401(k) Retirement Plan: With company match
  • Life Insurance: Basic life and AD&D coverage
  • Paid Time Off: Vacation, sick leave, and holidays
  • Professional Development: Training and continuing education opportunities

Detailed benefit information is provided during new hire orientation and is available from Human Resources.

7. Time Off Policies

Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue PTO based on length of service. PTO can be used for vacation, personal days, or illness. PTO must be requested and approved in advance except in cases of emergency.

Holidays

CollagenDirect observes the following paid holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Day After Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and New Year's Eve.

Medical Leave

Employees may be eligible for leave under the Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) or applicable state laws. Contact HR for more information.

8. Performance Management

Performance Reviews

Formal performance reviews are conducted annually. Your manager will provide ongoing feedback throughout the year to help you succeed and grow in your role.

Professional Development

We encourage continuous learning and professional growth. Employees may be eligible for training programs, conferences, and educational assistance.

9. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to:

  • Conduct themselves professionally at all times
  • Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect
  • Follow all company policies and procedures
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect company and customer confidential information
  • Avoid conflicts of interest
  • Report any suspected violations of policy or law

10. HIPAA & Confidentiality

‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENT

All employees are Business Associates under HIPAA and must comply with the Business Associate Agreement and HIPAA Rules. Violations can result in severe penalties and immediate termination.

Key Requirements:

  • Access PHI only when necessary for your job duties
  • Never discuss patient information in public or on social media
  • Use secure, encrypted communication for PHI
  • Lock away documents and secure your workstation
  • Report any suspected breaches within 24 hours
  • Complete annual HIPAA training

For questions or to report incidents, contact the Privacy Officer at privacy@collagendirect.health.

11. Technology & Equipment

Acceptable Use

Company technology and equipment (computers, phones, email, internet) are provided for business purposes. Personal use should be minimal and not interfere with work responsibilities.

Security

Employees must use strong passwords, never share login credentials, and report lost or stolen devices immediately. Company systems may be monitored for security and compliance purposes.

Social Media

Do not post confidential company or customer information on social media. Do not represent yourself as speaking on behalf of CollagenDirect without authorization.

12. Workplace Safety

CollagenDirect is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. Employees are expected to follow all safety policies and report any unsafe conditions or accidents immediately to their supervisor or HR.

Drug-Free Workplace

CollagenDirect maintains a drug-free workplace. The use, possession, or distribution of illegal drugs or alcohol on company premises or during work hours is strictly prohibited.

13. Anti-Harassment Policy

CollagenDirect prohibits harassment of any kind, including harassment based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected characteristic.

If you experience or witness harassment, report it immediately to your supervisor, HR, or any member of management. All complaints will be investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports harassment is strictly prohibited.

14. Disciplinary Action

CollagenDirect may use progressive discipline (verbal warning, written warning, suspension, termination) when addressing policy violations or performance issues. However, the company reserves the right to skip steps or proceed directly to termination for serious violations.

Immediate termination may result from: HIPAA violations, theft, violence, harassment, insubordination, falsification of records, or other serious misconduct.

15. Termination of Employment

Resignation

Employees who wish to resign are requested to provide two weeks' written notice to allow for a smooth transition.

Return of Company Property

Upon termination, employees must return all company property, including computers, phones, keys, access badges, and documents containing confidential or PHI information.

Final Paycheck

Final paychecks will be issued in accordance with applicable state law.

16. Acknowledgment Form

All employees must sign the Employee Handbook Acknowledgment Form confirming they have received, read, and understood the policies in this handbook.
