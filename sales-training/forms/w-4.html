Department of the Treasury

Internal Revenue Service

OMB No. 1545-0074

Employee's Withholding Certificate

Complete Form W-4 so that your employer can withhold the correct federal income tax from your pay.

Give Form W-4 to your employer.

Your withholding is subject to review by the IRS.

Step 1: Enter Personal Information

(d) Filing Status (Check only one box)

Step 2: Multiple Jobs or Spouse Works

Complete this step if you (1) hold more than one job at a time, or (2) are married filing jointly and your spouse also works. The correct amount of withholding depends on income earned from all of these jobs.

⚠️ Do only ONE of the following:

  • Option (a): Reserved for most accurate withholding (use online estimator or worksheet)
  • Option (b): Use if there are only TWO jobs total
  • Option (c): If there are more than two jobs, use the estimator at www.irs.gov/W4App

TIP: If you (or your spouse) have self-employment income, use the estimator at www.irs.gov/W4App.

Important Reminders:

  • Complete Steps 1-5 on or before your first day of employment
  • If you have multiple jobs or your spouse works, complete Step 2 carefully

  • You can submit a new W-4 anytime your personal or financial situation changes
